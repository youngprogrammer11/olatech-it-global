Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Who we are

Olatech IT Global is a technology company based in Lagos, Nigeria. We provide web development, software development, IT consulting, digital marketing, and branding services to businesses in Nigeria and internationally.

When this policy says "we", "us", or "Olatech", it means Olatech IT Global. When it says "you", it means anyone who uses our website or contacts us.

What information we collect

We only collect information that you give to us directly. This happens when you:

  • Fill in our contact form — we collect your name, email address, phone number, and the message you send
  • Send us an email directly
  • Message us on WhatsApp or social media

We also collect basic usage data automatically through Google Analytics, which includes:

  • Which pages you visit and for how long
  • Your general location (country or city level only)
  • The device and browser you use
  • How you found our website (Google, WhatsApp link, direct, etc.)

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as your financial details, ID numbers, or passwords.

How we use your information

We use the information you give us to:

  • Reply to your enquiries and discuss your project
  • Send you a quote or proposal
  • Communicate with you about work we are doing for you
  • Improve our website based on how visitors use it

We do not sell your information to anyone. We do not use your information for advertising. We do not add you to any mailing list without your permission.

How we store your information

Messages sent through our contact form are stored in a secure MongoDB database hosted on MongoDB Atlas. Access to this database is restricted to authorised staff only.

We keep your contact details for as long as we have an active business relationship with you. If you ask us to delete your information, we will do so within 30 days.

Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies — small text files stored in your browser. These cookies do not identify you personally.

You can turn off Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third party services

Our website uses the following third party services:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Resend — sends email notifications when you submit our contact form. Resend Privacy Policy
  • MongoDB Atlas — stores contact form submissions securely. MongoDB Privacy Policy
  • Render — hosts our website. Render Privacy Policy

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask us what information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct information that is wrong
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Children

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to check this page occasionally.

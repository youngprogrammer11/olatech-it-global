About Olatech

Started in Nigeria.
Working everywhere.

We started in Lagos, Nigeria in 2017. Today we work with businesses in Africa, Europe, and North America. We are a technology company that builds websites, software, and digital tools. Some of our clients are startups. Others are large companies. All of them want the same thing: work that gets done right.

Our team covers design, engineering, and strategy. We do not pass your project between departments. The people you talk to are the people who build your product.

Our Mission

To give every business access to good technology. We keep our prices fair, our process clear, and our work honest.

Our Vision

To be the go-to technology company for African businesses that want to compete on a global stage.

Good Work Only Clear Communication On Time Delivery Fair Pricing Long-term Thinking No Shortcuts
How we work

Five steps. No surprises.

01

Discovery

We learn what you need, who your users are, and what success looks like for your project.

02

Strategy

We write a clear plan with the tech stack, timeline, and budget. You approve it before anything starts.

03

Design

We build and show you the designs. You give feedback. We only write code once you are happy with what it looks like.

04

Build

We build in short sprints and show you progress every week. You can give feedback at any point.

05

Launch and Support

We test everything, launch your product, and stay available for fixes and improvements after go-live.
